CHARLOTTE'S WEB

"I want to stay alive, right here in my comfortable manure pile with all
my friends. I want to breathe the beautiful air and lie in the
beautiful sun."
"You're certainly making a beautiful noise," snapped the old sheep.
"I don't want to die!" screamed Wilbur, throwing himself to the ground.
"You shall not die," said Charlotte, briskly.
"What? Really?" cried Wilbur. "Who's going to save me?"
"I am," said Charlotte.
"How?" asked Wilbur.
"That remains to be seen. But I am going to save you, and I want you
to
quiet down immediately. You're carrying on in a childish way. Stop
your crying! I can't stand hysterics."
