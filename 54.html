CHARLOTTE'S WEB
54
"Charlotte?" he said, softly.
"Yes, Wilbur?"
"I don't want to die."
"Of course you don't," said Charlotte in a comforting voice.
"I just love it here in the barn," said Wilbur. "I love everything
about this place."
"Of course you do," said Charlotte. "We all do."
The goose appeared, followed by her seven goslings. They thrust their
little necks out and kept up a musical whistling, like a tiny troupe of
pipers. Wilbur listened to the sound with love in his heart.
"Charlotte?" he said.
"Yes?" said the spider.
"Were you serious when you promised you would keep them from killing
me?"
"I was never more serious in my life. I am not going to let you die,
Wilbur."
"How are you going to save me?" asked Wilbur, whose curiosity was
very
strong on this point.
