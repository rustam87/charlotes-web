CHARLOTTE'S WEB
74
"Here!" said the pig.
"Gander?"
"Here, here, here!" said the gander.
"You sound like three ganders," muttered Charlotte. "Why can't you just
say 'here'? Why do you have to repeat everything?"
"It's my idio-idio-idiosyncrasy," replied the gander.
"Goose?" said Charlotte.
"Here, here, here!" said the goose. Charlotte glared at her.
"Goslings, one through seven?"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!"
"Bee-bee-bee!" said the goslings.
